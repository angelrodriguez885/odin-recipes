Scrambled Eggs

Description

An egg dish that blends the whites and yolks of eggs with milk and seasonings to be cooked in an open pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Cottage Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon Olive Oil

Steps

  • Mix all of your ingredients together in a bowl, blender, or food processor (use a blender or food processor if you don't like the texture of cottage cheese)
  • Take out a stovetop pan or griddle and turn your burner on medium heat
  • Coat your pan or griddle with your Olive Oil, Butter, or Cooking Spray
  • Pour your mix in and cook your eggs until your desired consistency (keep them moving as you cook them for the best result)
